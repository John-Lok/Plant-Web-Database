Introduction: 
This project's original purpose is to aid my mother's PhD assignment of creating a database to record and store information about the Malaysian indigenous people’s (Orang Asli) uses of plants around their area. My mother majors in the biology and chemistry field of science, whereas I graduated from secondary school with computer science as one of my elective subjects. Hence, this is why I have taken on this project as a side project while working closely with my mother and her Computer Science supervisor from Universiti Tenaga Nasional (UniTEN). 

As of the starting date of this project, although I have studied computer science for 2 years in secondary school, I still lack sufficient knowledge to make a proper and fully functioning website. So I have taken on this project to also improve on my programming, coding and system designing skills. As I slowly develop this website, I will be learning new concepts, syntax and functions of programming languages (primarily HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and MySQL) and the overall work flow to be able to design and create any website.
